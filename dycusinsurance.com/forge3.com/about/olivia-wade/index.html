Meet Olivia Wade.

Say hello to our Client Experience Manager.

”I am ready to face any challenges that might be foolish enough to face me.

Olivia WadeClient Experience Manager | Forge3, Ltd.

Born in New Jersey, I moved to Pennsylvania to attend DeSales University for Television and Film. Throughout my school career, I was on the track and field throwing team and, in college, helped start the DeSales women’s rugby team, which is still going strong today. After graduation, I stayed in Pennsylvania and worked in a handful of different types of customer service roles before I found my home here at Forge3. One of my favorite jobs I had right out of school was being a family photographer. The best days were pet days. Who wouldn’t want to spend a day taking pictures of adorable children and pets?

In my free time, I enjoy watching hockey and binge-watching my favorite shows. My recent favorites are The Witcher and The Mandalorian, but you can never go wrong by rewatching The Office. I love to cross-stitch, knit, paint, take photos, and do other crafty things.

My boyfriend, Chris, and I have been together for almost three years now. We love to play video games and go to concerts. Together we live in Bethlehem with our two kitties, Casso and Adore. Adore, the youngest, was rescued from the parking lot at Chris’ work where they made doors. So that is how we got the name Adore (a door)!
